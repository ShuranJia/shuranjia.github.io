Why Join

Baseball is a team sport that promotes both physical fitness and friendship. In our school baseball team, there are lots of activities. Not only the on-campus training, we will also organize a week-long off-campus training picnic. During the off-campus training, we can train carefully during the day, and we can sing together, eat barbecue, watch movies and other colorful activities at night. Of course, training is also divided into physical training and skill training, which not only increases the physical ability but also improves the skill greatly. Every training session is full of laughter. We all welcome students to join the baseball team.
